Colosseum's Rules

  • the game can start with any # of players, each with 3 health. Each turn a player can choose to:
    • attack one other player
    • block incoming attacks
    • counter incoming attacks
    • heal
    • repair your shield
  • If you’re attacked when you’re not blocking or countering you lose 1 health per incoming attack.
  • If you block you take no damage from incoming attacks. If you block an attack your shield breaks and you can’t block again until you spend a turn repairing your shield.
  • If you counter you take no damage from incoming attacks and you also deal 1 damage to anybody that attacks you that turn. However, if nobody attacks you, you take 1 damage.
  • If you heal and nobody attacks you you regain 1 health. If you’re attacked while healing your heal is cancelled and you take damage as usual. You can’t heal when at 4 health.
